Captains log: Stardate : 23/05/18

Welcome!
Glad to see you're still reading my blog!



What a fantastic end to the start of the weekend, and end to our first week!

We spent today continuing with our lodash katas. Where we work with each to get a greater understanding of how certain methods work by actually designing them ourselves!
This is all great and exciting, whilst still challenging, with the reward of success when one beats the challenge!
Both my partner and I feel it is a great insight into looking at things under the hood.
We had a short presentation too by some cohorts who are further along with the process.

It was exciting to see what they had made. It was all front end website design. I think it really motivated a lot of us and got us really anticipating what's to come. For us to be at that stage in only a couple of weeks is going to be amazing!!!! As you may be able to tell I do like the front-end aspect. Possibly because I can directly see my code in action live on screen.

A really pleasant surprise was getting handed a beer around 5 to welcome the weekend in, before we finished at 6. As well as this an invitation to go down to the local bar after was a really nice touch. It helped me feel included and valued really early on!

All glasses were raised in acknowledgement of the great decision we all made to be a Northcoder!!!
