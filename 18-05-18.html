Captains log: Stardate : 18/05/18

Welcome!
Hello, you've made it to my first ever blog!



Wow! I thought yesterday was tough. I guess I should have realised when it said on the testimonials.

So we got in and started at 8.30. We spent an hour and half just on our own private katas today. Before a lecture held by Paul.
He is really good and engaging, and I think his constant beaming smile settles all our nerves when we - already- are having problems we have to work hard to tackle.
The lecture didn't seem as long as yesterday's and we were soon upstairs to tackle 1)the pair katas we were last working on yesterday and 2) a new set of pairing katas. These new katas were very tough. Challenging and sometimes gruelling but still fun as Nic and I felt a great sense achievement when we got it right.
I also think we got a better hold on working with our tests first before starting our code. And it really did help!

After our break at 1 we got back in and got back to work. Tackling what we didn't finish. Not to say that we did finish everything.
